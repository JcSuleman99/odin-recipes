Hot Honey Pepperoni Pizza

Ingedients

  • 1/2 cup of Honey
  • 1 tbsp Dried chili Flakes
  • 1/2 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  • 2 COBS Bread Pizza Bases
  • 1/2 cup Pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded Mozarella
  • 1 cup Pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup Hot Honey

Directions

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 425F
  2. Add your honey, chilli flakes, and apple cider vinegar to a pot and bring to a simmer over medium heat.
  3. As soon as you start to see bubbles, take the pot off the heat and let the chili flakes infuse with the honey for 5-10 minutes. The longer you let it infuse, the spicier it will be
  4. When ready, place a fine mesh sieve/strainer over a bowl or glass and pour the honey mixture into the sieve. This will strain the chili flakes from the hot honey. If you like spice you can add a few of the chili flakes back into the honey! Set this aside.
  5. Add pizza sauce to your COBS Bread Pizza Bases, then top with cheese (we like using a mozarella blend)
  6. Add pepperoni on top, then bake on a parchment-lined baking tray for 12-15 minutes, or until the cheese is nice and golden.
  7. Slice your pizza then drizzle with the hot honey and enjoy!
